DIPLOMA IN OFFICE MANAGEMENT TECHNOLOGY




The Diploma in Office Management Technology (DOMT) is a three-year ladderized program that offers course that emphasis on the development of the students practical skills in book and records keeping, administrative support, and information, communication, technology and events management.

The program is designed to equip students with secretarial/office skills for employment in various fields of endeavour and also in vocational development. Students of this programme are exposed to course in their special areas as well as courses in general education



OBJECTIVE


The program provides the ability/training necessary to perform successfully in the many and varied clerical, secretarial and office administrative position. The Diploma in Office Management Technology (DOMT) prepares students for careers in office management and administration, particularly in medical and legal office management.



ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS


  1. Applicant's photo (JPEG file)
  2. General Weighted Average (GWA) in Grades 10 and 11
  3. Scanned Grade 10 Report Card (JPEG file)
  4. Scanned Grade 11 Report Card (JPEG file)
  5. PSA Birth Certificate
  6. X-ray for Health Clearance
  7. Certification of Good Moral Character
